Logo

The Lighthouse woodcut was created by David Lazurus. The handmade details reflect the school's artistic, hands-on ethos. Some of the linework is quite fine, so it's important to follow several guidelines when using the logo online:

  1. Minimum dimensions: 140px wide x 54px tall
  2. The lighthouse mark can be used alone, but no smaller than 50px x 50px
  3. Try to keep a margin around the logo and never have it bumping against another graphic or text. The recommended amount of margin is equal to the width of the "L" in Lighthouse.

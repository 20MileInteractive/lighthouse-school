The Admissions Process at Lighthouse School

Thank you for your interest in Nantucket Lighthouse School!

If you are interested in learning more about our program for your preschool – 8th grader, I encourage you to join us for an Open House or to call me to set up a tour. Nothing conveys our rich educational offering better than a visit to the school when it is in session.

Here’s how the admissions process works:
  1. Attend an Open House or set up a tour and request a packet of information about the school.
  2. Submit a completed application. (Download application PDF.)
  3. Interview for admissions. The interview is typically 45 minutes in length and gives you a chance to ask any questions you may have after having attended an Open House or taken a tour, and it gives our Head of School an opportunity to meet your child and determine where he or she would best fit in our school.
  4. After the interview is concluded, and if you are interested, your child is invited to visit the school for a full or half day.
  5. Admissions notification is mailed out with a commitment contract.

Meet Our Admissions Director

Hi, I'm Cheryl Evans. I'm the Admissions Director for the Lighthouse School. I'm ready to help you learn more about us. Please feel free to contact me with any questions you may have or to set up a tour. I look forward to hearing from you!

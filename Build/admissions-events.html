Admission Events

Thank you for your interest in Nantucket Lighthouse School!

Attending an Open House is the best way to learn more about Lighthouse School. Please join NLS Head of School Emily Miller for a tour of the classrooms and a discussion of the curriculum utilizing student work. There are two Open House dates scheduled during which NLS teachers will be available to ask questions and find out more about specific classrooms (see below). Please note that these sessions are intended for adults. Space is limited and so we ask you to pre-register for these sessions by calling our office at 508.228.0427 or registering online

Open House Schedule:
  • Thursday, October 17, 11:00 a.m.- 12 p.m. (HOS)
  • Saturday, November 23, 11:00 a.m. (HOS and NLS Faculty)
  • Thursday, January 9, 5:30 p.m. (HOS and NLS Faculty)

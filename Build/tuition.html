Tuition and Financial Aid

Tuition is set each year by the board of trustees of Nantucket Lighthouse School. Tuition for the 2013-2014 school year:

Small School
$12,400, or $344 a week over the 36 weeks of the school year

All Other Grades
$13,700, or $380 a week over the 36 weeks of the school year

Payment Plans

We understand that different families may have different financial needs. That's why we offer several payment plans to best fit your budget:

  • In one full payment by February
  • After a commitment fee of $750, with one payment by March
  • After a commitment fee of $750, in four payments, April through August
  • After a commitment fee of $750, in ten payments, April through January
Financial Aid

Financial Aid is available to qualifying families who complete an application and submit tax records. Awards are generally 20- 30% of tuition. Learn more about the process of applying for financial aid here.

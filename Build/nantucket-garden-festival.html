Nantucket Garden Festival

July 25 – 27, 2013 • Pumpkin Pond Farm

With the guidance of alumni trustee and avid gardener, Connie Umberger, the Nantucket Lighthouse School, long a believer in using gardens and gardening as an educational tool, developed the Nantucket Garden Festival in 2009.

The 5th Annual Nantucket Garden Festival (NGF) will be held July 25th-July 27th, 2013 at Pumpkin Pond Farm. The Nantucket Garden Festival provides a fresh approach to gardening, inspiring all generations of gardeners while maintaining a pledge of conservation and sustainability. Nantucket has a myriad of extraordinary gardens and is a premiere destination for any traveling horticulturist. The NGF captures the romance and allure of gardening on Nantucket with an opening night bash, A Summer Tea with George Schoellkopf of Hollister House Garden and lecture with best-selling author Amy Stewart. In addition, there are unique children’s activities, community challenges and exclusive garden tours.

The Nantucket Garden Festival has enjoyed the support of ReMain Nantucket for the last four years, and this year is pleased to present a challenge match from ReMain. For every dollar we raise in underwriting for the event, ReMain will match it, up to $15,000! If you are interested in supporting, participating or attending the Nantucket Garden Festival, please contact Director of Development, Joy Margolis at 508-228-0427.
